Transfer Pricing

We can assist you by preparing the Master File and/or Local File in accordance to the Indonesian tax regulations and OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines.

EXPERT ASSISTANCE FOR COMPLIANCE, DISPUTES, AND STRATEGIC PLANNING

Transfer Pricing continues to be one of the most important tax issues both for the Tax Authority and taxpayer. Tax Authority are focusing more intensely on transfer pricing issues, and companies are facing increased scrutiny over their transfer pricing policy. Furthermore, the regulations surrounding transfer pricing are constantly shifting and becoming more complex. It has become more critical for Taxpayer to consider how best to manage their transfer pricing practice in this environment.

Our team have extensive knowledge of transfer pricing rules and handle transfer pricing dispute. We can assist you to provide a comprehensive assistance from compliance and documentation, audit and dispute process, as well as work with you for the transfer pricing strategy and planning.
